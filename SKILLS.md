# SKILLS
## Chronicle of the Central Plains — Skill Compendium

---

## Overview

Skills grant a **+2 bonus** to relevant d20 rolls at Basic tier, scaling to +4 at Advanced and +6 at Mastery. Each skill lists the primary **Attribute** used for rolls and the two or three **Synergy** combinations that unlock combo abilities when paired with other skills.

**Skill Tiers:**
- **Basic (Level 1):** +2 bonus.
- **Advanced (Level 3):** +4 bonus and a named special ability.
- **Mastery (Level 5):** +6 bonus and a unique passive.

**Magic skills** (Arcana, Faith) follow the standard tier system but have separate acquisition rules — see RULES.md (§ Special Skills) and MAGIC.md for full details.

## COMBAT SKILLS

---

### Blade
**Attribute:** Finesse

Proficiency with swords, daggers, and all edged weapons. The first skill most frontier soldiers learn and the last one that keeps them breathing.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Standard attack and parry rolls with edged weapons. |
| Advanced | +4 | Precision Strike | Once per combat, declare a precise attack before rolling. On a success, target a specific body location — weapon arm, throat, knee. Injury lands exactly where intended. |
| Mastery | +6 | Read the Blade (passive) | At the start of each combat round, the GM tells you one thing your opponent is about to do — a feint, a guard drop, an overreach. You may act on it or ignore it. |

**Synergies:**
- **Blade + Stealth → Backstab:** A flanking or unseen attack ignores armour on a success.
- **Blade + Athletics → Duelist's Step:** Never take terrain penalties in melee.

---

### Archery
**Attribute:** Finesse

Bows, crossbows, and thrown weapons. Essential on the frontier where the dead don't slow down and you want fifteen feet of air between you and them.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Standard ranged attack rolls. Effective range: medium (no penalty). Short = +2. Long = -2. Extreme = -4. |
| Advanced | +4 | Still Shot | May spend one full action doing nothing — no moving, no talking — to remove all range and movement penalties on the next shot. |
| Mastery | +6 | Nocked (passive) | Always treated as having a readied shot. May fire as a reaction to incoming attackers before they reach melee range. Once per scene. |

**Synergies:**
- **Archery + Tracking → Ambush:** Set a ranged ambush position; first shot made at advantage.
- **Archery + Stealth → Ghost Archer:** No position reveal on a clean success.

---

### Knife Fighting
**Attribute:** Finesse

Close-quarters fighting with short blades, improvised weapons, and the willingness to get ugly. Common among thieves, soldiers who lost their swords, and anyone who has spent time in city slums.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Attack and defend with daggers, short blades, and improvised weapons. |
| Advanced | +4 | Dirty Trick | Before rolling an attack, declare a dirty trick — sand in the eyes, knee to the groin, bitten thumb. On a success the opponent takes a -2 penalty to their next roll. |
| Mastery | +6 | Always Armed (passive) | Never considered unarmed. Can draw and attack in the same action. Concealed blades are never found in casual searches. |

**Synergies:**
- **Knife Fighting + Blade → Dual Wield:** Attack twice per round; second attack at -2.
- **Knife Fighting + Lockpicking → Concealment:** May hide a blade even during pat-downs; DC raised by 5.

---

### Unarmed Combat
**Attribute:** Might

Brawling, wrestling, grappling. What you fall back on when everything else is gone. Surprisingly effective against the undead, if you don't mind the smell.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Punch, kick, and grapple. Grapple on a success pins opponent — both parties roll Might next round to break or maintain. |
| Advanced | +4 | Iron Grip | Grapple cannot be broken by the opponent's Finesse rolls, only Might. While grappling, the opponent cannot cast spells or use ranged weapons. |
| Mastery | +6 | Wall of Flesh (passive) | May absorb one hit per scene that would cause a Moderate or lesser injury, reducing it to a Minor injury through sheer physical resilience. |

**Synergies:**
- **Unarmed Combat + Endurance → Punishing Pace:** Each round a grapple is maintained, the opponent takes a Minor torso injury from exhaustion.
- **Unarmed Combat + Intimidation → Dominating Presence:** Breaking free of your grapple requires DC 18 instead of DC 15.

---

### Shield & Spear
**Attribute:** Might

The oldest formation fighting in the world. Wall shields, short spears, and the discipline to hold a line while the thing coming toward you does not care about pain.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Block incoming attacks with a shield. A shield reduces injury severity by one tier on a successful block roll. |
| Advanced | +4 | Hold the Line | While stationary, grant +1 to adjacent allies' defence rolls. Cannot be knocked back or repositioned by anything below Impossible difficulty. |
| Mastery | +6 | Bulwark (passive) | May take damage intended for an adjacent ally once per round. The injury lands on your shield arm instead of the target. |

**Synergies:**
- **Shield & Spear + Endurance → Tortoise:** Can hold a defensive formation for hours without penalty.
- **Shield & Spear + Intimidation → Wall of Steel:** Enemies must pass a DC 12 Resolve check to charge your position.

## SURVIVAL SKILLS

---

### Stealth
**Attribute:** Finesse

Moving without sound, hiding without being found, becoming part of the background. Worth twice its weight on the frontier where most things that want to kill you hunt by movement.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Hide, move silently, remain undetected in dim conditions. |
| Advanced | +4 | Melt Away | May attempt to hide during combat even if enemies know your general location — not your exact position. DC 15. Success removes you from immediate targeting for one round. |
| Mastery | +6 | Shadowmeld (passive) | In low light or darkness, you require no roll to remain hidden while stationary. Moving still requires a roll, but only on a Critical Failure are you spotted. |

**Synergies:**
- **Stealth + Blade → Backstab:** A flanking or unseen attack ignores armour on a success.
- **Stealth + Tracking → Hunter's Patience:** May set ambushes that force enemies into disadvantaged positions.

---

### Tracking
**Attribute:** Cunning

Reading the land — footprints, broken branches, old fire rings, the way the mud has been disturbed. Tells you who came through, when, how many, and whether they knew they were being followed.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Follow trails in clear conditions. Identify creature type, rough number, and approximate time. |
| Advanced | +4 | Sign Reading | From a campsite, abandoned building, or fresh scene: determine what happened there with a DC 15 roll — how many people, who was afraid, what they were carrying, which direction they fled. |
| Mastery | +6 | Haunted Eye (passive) | You always notice when you are being followed. The GM alerts you in secret when you have a tail. You know you're being watched. You do not always know by what. |

**Synergies:**
- **Tracking + Stealth → Hunter's Patience:** May set ambushes that force enemies into disadvantaged positions.
- **Tracking + Wilderness Survival → Pathfinder:** Travel time through difficult terrain is halved; the group never gets lost.

---

### Wilderness Survival
**Attribute:** Cunning

Finding water, making fire, identifying safe food, building a shelter that doesn't collapse, knowing when the weather is about to kill you. Required knowledge for anyone who spends time more than a day's walk from a settlement.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Forage, camp, and navigate safely. The party can make do without provisions for 1d3 days per roll. |
| Advanced | +4 | Hard Country | Forced marches and extreme weather no longer force penalty rolls on the character. Allies within earshot gain +1 to related Endurance checks. |
| Mastery | +6 | Of the Land (passive) | In wilderness environments, always know approximately where you are, what direction you're moving, and what the land will look like for the next half-day's travel. Cannot be lost. |

**Synergies:**
- **Wilderness Survival + Tracking → Pathfinder:** Travel time through difficult terrain is halved; the group never gets lost.
- **Wilderness Survival + Herbalism → Forager's Eye:** May locate medicinal plants even in winter or arid terrain.

---

### Herbalism
**Attribute:** Cunning

Identifying plants, preparing poultices, brewing draughts, understanding which root will stop bleeding and which one will stop a heart. The frontier physician's most relied-upon skill.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Prepare basic field medicines. A prepared poultice allows Minor and Moderate injury recovery without a physician (functions as field dressing). |
| Advanced | +4 | Physician's Hand | Treatment functions at Garrison Physic standard. May prepare antidotes for common poisons and draughts that grant +2 to Endurance rolls for one scene. |
| Mastery | +6 | Root Knowledge (passive) | May prepare compounds that function as Skilled Physician treatments. One per three-day rest period. The materials are rare — the GM determines availability. |

**Synergies:**
- **Herbalism + Alchemy → Distillation:** Can produce more potent versions of any prepared compound at the cost of rare ingredients.
- **Herbalism + Wilderness Survival → Forager's Eye:** May locate medicinal plants even in winter or arid terrain.

---

### Endurance
**Attribute:** Might

The capacity to keep going after everything else runs out. Long marches, hard labour, cold nights without shelter, blood loss. Endurance is what separates those who make it back from those who don't.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Forced march, exposure, and physical labour rolls. Recover one additional Minor injury with each Full Rest. |
| Advanced | +4 | Second Wind | Once per scene: after failing a Grit Check, immediately re-roll with advantage. Cannot be used again until a Scene Rest. |
| Mastery | +6 | Iron Gut (passive) | Grit Check threshold raises by one — you must accumulate four qualifying injuries before Grit Checks begin, not three. Untreated Moderate injuries do not worsen on failures. |

**Synergies:**
- **Endurance + Unarmed Combat → Punishing Pace:** Each round a grapple is maintained, the opponent takes a Minor torso injury from exhaustion.
- **Endurance + Athletics → Hardened:** Severe injuries from physical exertion are reduced to Moderate.

---

### Athletics
**Attribute:** Finesse

Running, climbing, swimming, jumping. The body used as an instrument of movement. More valuable than it sounds when you're running from something that cannot tire.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Running, climbing, and swimming rolls. Clear obstacles without penalty that would otherwise require rolls. |
| Advanced | +4 | Burst | Once per scene: move at double speed for one round. May be used to escape melee range without triggering a free attack from the opponent. |
| Mastery | +6 | Fluid Motion (passive) | Never penalised by difficult terrain in open environments. May take any physical movement action as a free action once per round. |

**Synergies:**
- **Athletics + Blade → Duelist's Step:** Never take terrain penalties in melee.
- **Athletics + Stealth → Ghost Run:** May move at full speed without Stealth penalty on a success.

## SOCIAL SKILLS

---

### Persuasion
**Attribute:** Cunning

Getting people to agree with you, do things for you, or believe things you want them to believe. Works better when you have something they want or they already trust you. Works worse when they're hungry and you're between them and food.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Standard social negotiation. Works on NPCs with neutral or positive disposition. |
| Advanced | +4 | Find the Lever | Before attempting persuasion, ask the GM one question about what this NPC wants most. You get a true answer. You can use it or not. |
| Mastery | +6 | Trusted Face (passive) | NPCs with whom you've had one prior positive interaction treat you as Allied by default. Hostile NPCs begin at Neutral rather than Hostile. |

**Synergies:**
- **Persuasion + Intimidation → Bad Shepherd:** May combine both to make offers the target fears refusing.
- **Persuasion + Lore → Informed Position:** Knowing the relevant history adds +2 to relevant persuasion rolls.

---

### Intimidation
**Attribute:** Might

Making things afraid of you. Or at least making them behave as though they are, which amounts to the same thing in practice. The fastest social skill on the frontier. Also the one that burns the most bridges.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Browbeat, threaten, and cow NPCs. Works on anyone with reason to fear you — which on the frontier is most people. |
| Advanced | +4 | Weight of Reputation | Your name precedes you. Once per session, invoke your reputation in lieu of a roll: the target simply assumes you can follow through on your threats. GM discretion on scope. |
| Mastery | +6 | Fear Made Manifest (passive) | When you enter a hostile situation, one NPC of your choice immediately re-evaluates their position. They may back down, flee, or attempt to negotiate. They do not fight you without reason. |

**Synergies:**
- **Intimidation + Persuasion → Bad Shepherd:** May combine both to make offers the target fears refusing.
- **Intimidation + Unarmed Combat → Dominating Presence:** Breaking free of your grapple requires DC 18 instead of DC 15.

---

### Deception
**Attribute:** Cunning

Lying, misdirecting, performing. The ability to hold a false face under pressure. Common in merchants, city officials, and anyone who has spent too long in a place where the truth is dangerous.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Tell convincing lies, adopt disguises, pass as someone else in low-scrutiny situations. |
| Advanced | +4 | The Long Game | Maintain a sustained false identity or deception for days or weeks without rolls — unless directly investigated or confronted with hard evidence. |
| Mastery | +6 | Seamless (passive) | You cannot be detected as lying by mundane means. Magical detection requires a DC 20 opposed Arcana check. Even then you feel the probe and know someone tried. |

**Synergies:**
- **Deception + Stealth → Vanishing Act:** After a deception is believed, may disappear without drawing suspicion.
- **Deception + Streetwise → Cover Story:** A prepared false background holds up to moderate investigation.

---

### Streetwise
**Attribute:** Cunning

Knowing how cities work from the bottom up — where to fence stolen goods, who the real power in a district is, which inn doesn't ask questions, how the guard is organised and where the holes are.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Navigate urban environments socially. Find black markets, avoided locations, and useful contacts within a city. |
| Advanced | +4 | Word on the Street | Within any city you've spent at least one day in, you may ask the GM for a rumour relevant to your current objective. It is true, partially true, or deliberately false — you won't know which without investigation. |
| Mastery | +6 | Invisible (passive) | In cities, you are never remarkable. Guards, merchants, and officials do not notice you unless given specific reason to. You move through checkpoints, markets, and crowds without incident. |

**Synergies:**
- **Streetwise + Deception → Cover Story:** A prepared false background holds up to moderate investigation.
- **Streetwise + Lockpicking → Inside Job:** May locate building schematics, guard rotations, and vulnerabilities for a fee.

---

### Command
**Attribute:** Resolve

The capacity to lead under pressure — to issue orders that are followed, to hold frightened people together, to be the stillness at the centre of a panicking crowd. Rare. More useful than a sword on most days.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Rally fleeing NPCs, issue combat orders that grant +1 to allies' rolls in a coordinated action. |
| Advanced | +4 | Rally | Once per scene: a group of friendly NPCs who have failed morale and are fleeing or frozen may immediately re-roll. Success means they hold. |
| Mastery | +6 | Centre of Gravity (passive) | Allied NPCs within earshot add +1 to all Resolve checks. Morale failures are delayed by one round in your presence — panic spreads less quickly. |

**Synergies:**
- **Command + Persuasion → Diplomatic Leverage:** Negotiations with political weight gain +2.
- **Command + Faith → Holy Standard:** When both are active, Rally costs nothing and affects the entire scene.

## KNOWLEDGE SKILLS

---

### Lore
**Attribute:** Cunning

Accumulated knowledge of history, creatures, places, and the old world. Knowing what something is before it kills you is valuable. Knowing what it was a thousand years ago is occasionally more so.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Identify common monsters, recall relevant history, recognise notable places and artefacts. |
| Advanced | +4 | Obscure Knowledge | May roll to know things other scholars would not — pre-imperial history, creature weaknesses not in common circulation, the significance of ruins and markings. |
| Mastery | +6 | Living Archive (passive) | The GM must tell you if you recognise anything significant in a new location — a creature's origin, a building's original purpose, the source of a magical effect. Once per scene, no roll required. |

**Synergies:**
- **Lore + Arcana → Esoteric:** Rolls to identify magical phenomena are made at advantage.
- **Lore + Faith → Theological Depth:** Prayers directed at historically significant sites gain a DC reduction of 2.

---

### Medicine
**Attribute:** Cunning

Formal training in anatomy, diagnosis, and treatment. The difference between Herbalism — which works in the field on common wounds — and Medicine is that Medicine handles the complicated things: organ damage, infection, fever, things that should have already killed you.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Treat injuries at Garrison Physic standard. Stabilise Critical injuries with DC 16 rather than DC 20. |
| Advanced | +4 | Diagnosis | Examine a patient for one minute and identify all current injuries, their severity, and any underlying conditions the GM has noted. No hidden wounds or cursed states from trauma are concealed from you. |
| Mastery | +6 | Master Surgeon (passive) | Treatment functions at Master Surgeon standard. Severe injuries clear in one day. Critical injuries improve to Severe in three days with proper facilities. |

**Synergies:**
- **Medicine + Herbalism → Full Physician:** Combined treatment can address both physical and systemic conditions simultaneously.
- **Medicine + Arcana → Arcane Anatomy:** May identify magically-induced injuries and the school of magic that caused them.

---

### Alchemy
**Attribute:** Cunning

The practical chemistry of the old world — distillation, compounding, the understanding of what happens when substances are combined under heat and pressure. Not magic. Not exactly not magic either.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Identify alchemical substances. Brew basic compounds — fire oil, mild toxins, rudimentary adhesives — given ingredients and time. |
| Advanced | +4 | Field Lab | May work without a proper laboratory using available materials. Results are somewhat less reliable: a DC 10 check to confirm the batch is stable before use. |
| Mastery | +6 | Volatile Mind (passive) | Always know the approximate alchemical composition of any substance you can examine. Can identify poisons by smell, recognise alchemical fire, and detect unstable magical residue. |

**Synergies:**
- **Alchemy + Herbalism → Distillation:** Can produce more potent versions of any prepared compound at the cost of rare ingredients.
- **Alchemy + Arcana → Reagent Craft:** Alchemical compounds can be used as spell foci, reducing arcane casting cost by one tier once per scene.

---

### Crafting
**Attribute:** Cunning

The making and repair of physical things — weapons, armour, tools, structural work. On the frontier, someone who can repair a palisade gate or forge a new bolt for a crossbow is worth more than someone who can swing a sword.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Repair damaged equipment. Craft simple tools and structures given materials and time. |
| Advanced | +4 | Improvise | May craft functional equipment from salvaged or inappropriate materials. A field repair with the wrong metal holds long enough to matter. DC 13 for anything structurally demanding. |
| Mastery | +6 | Dwarven Touch (passive) | Objects you craft or repair last twice as long and require half the normal maintenance. Your armour repairs restore one tier of injury protection lost to damage. |

**Synergies:**
- **Crafting + Alchemy → Treated Gear:** May alchemically treat crafted items for fire resistance, undead repulsion, or edge retention.
- **Crafting + Endurance → Field Repairs:** May patch armour or weapons during a Scene Rest rather than requiring downtime.

## CRIMINAL SKILLS

---

### Lockpicking
**Attribute:** Finesse

Bypass locks, bars, and sealed mechanisms. What the city calls a criminal skill. What the frontier calls knowing how to get into an abandoned building that might have food and medicine before the thing outside gets you.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Pick common locks, lift simple bars, bypass standard mechanisms. |
| Advanced | +4 | Ghost Entry | May pick a lock or bypass a door without leaving any sign of entry. Nothing is disturbed. The owner may never know. |
| Mastery | +6 | Master's Touch (passive) | No lock below Exceptional quality requires a roll. Exceptional locks require DC 14. Magical wards must be identified before bypassing — they are a separate Arcana roll. |

**Synergies:**
- **Lockpicking + Streetwise → Inside Job:** May locate building schematics, guard rotations, and vulnerabilities for a fee.
- **Lockpicking + Stealth → Second Story:** May enter buildings via upper floors and unusual entry points without noise or evidence.

---

### Sleight of Hand
**Attribute:** Finesse

Palming objects, picking pockets, planting evidence, hiding things on your person. A city skill that becomes surprisingly useful when you need to get something past a guard or make a weapon disappear.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Pickpocket, palm small objects, and conceal items on your person without detection. |
| Advanced | +4 | The Hand is Faster | May lift an object from a person during normal conversation or while they are distracted, with no roll required if they have no reason to be guarded around you. |
| Mastery | +6 | Nothing to See (passive) | Even if caught in the act, onlookers are uncertain what they saw. Accusations must be supported by a Cunning check from the accuser to overcome your plausible deniability. |

**Synergies:**
- **Sleight of Hand + Deception → Misdirect:** Opponents who fail a Cunning check act on false information about what you're carrying.
- **Sleight of Hand + Knife Fighting → Concealment:** May hide a blade even during pat-downs; DC raised by 5.

---

### Scavenging
**Attribute:** Cunning

Finding useful things in places that seem empty. Knowing what's worth taking, where people hide things, and how to identify value in wreckage. Essential knowledge in the recolonisation zone where the ruins still have useful things in them.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Search a location for useful items. Find what's there if it isn't actively hidden. |
| Advanced | +4 | Vulture's Eye | In any abandoned building or ruin, the GM tells you the most valuable item in the location at the start of your search — not where it is, just that it exists and roughly what it is. |
| Mastery | +6 | Treasure Sense (passive) | Always know if a location has already been picked clean before committing time to searching it. Never waste a search on an empty room. |

**Synergies:**
- **Scavenging + Wilderness Survival → Wasteland Adaptation:** Can maintain the party's supplies from ruins and abandoned farmsteads indefinitely.
- **Scavenging + Alchemy → Component Hunt:** May locate alchemical ingredients in ruins and old buildings.

---

### Poisoncraft
**Attribute:** Cunning

The preparation and application of substances that cause harm through contact, ingestion, or inhalation. Illegal in most cities. Entirely understandable on a frontier where you might need something that kills quietly.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Prepare basic contact and ingestion poisons. Apply them to weapons. Identify common poisons. |
| Advanced | +4 | Tailor Made | May prepare poisons for specific effects — paralysis, sleep, weakness, confusion. Each requires a DC 13 Cunning roll to compound and appropriate ingredients. |
| Mastery | +6 | Toxic Palate (passive) | Immune to all poisons you have personally handled. Others in your presence take -2 to saves against your compounds. |

**Synergies:**
- **Poisoncraft + Herbalism → Botanical Knowledge:** May derive rare poisons from plants found only in specific regions.
- **Poisoncraft + Sleight of Hand → The Long Pour:** May poison a drink or meal without detection even under observation.

## MAGIC SKILLS

*Magic skills follow separate acquisition rules. See RULES.md (§ Special Skills) and MAGIC.md for full casting rules, costs, and consequences.*

---

### Faith
**Attribute:** Resolve
**Acquisition:** May be taken at character creation by any character who worships a god. Starting value equals the Resolve modifier (minimum +0). Declare your god and one vow at session zero.

Devotion to the Fate — or another power that listens. Faith is the only magic available at character creation. It does not come from you. It comes from what you believe in, and how much of yourself you put behind it.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Prayers and minor blessings. Light that keeps dead things uneasy. Words that calm the frightened. The god is distant but listening. |
| Advanced | +4 | Miracles | Direct divine intervention becomes possible. Divine Tests trigger more frequently. The god is paying attention now. |
| Mastery | +6 | Constant Attention (passive) | The god's gaze is never fully off you. This is not comfortable. But it means that in moments of absolute extremity, the god acts — at a cost the GM determines. |

**Losing Faith:** Breaking vows or acting against your god's tenets reduces Faith by one tier. Losing Basic Faith entirely means the god has turned away — no further faith rolls until atonement.

**Synergies:**
- **Faith + Command → Holy Standard:** When both are active, Rally costs nothing and affects the entire scene.
- **Faith + Lore → Theological Depth:** Prayers directed at historically significant sites gain a DC reduction of 2.
- **Faith + Medicine → Laying On:** Once per scene, may attempt to halt an injury's worsening through prayer alone. DC 14 Resolve.

---

### Arcana
**Attribute:** Cunning
**Acquisition:** Cannot be taken at character creation. Must be found, learned from a dying sorcerer, extracted from a grimoire, or paid for in ways that leave marks. Those who go looking for it rarely find something clean.

The knowledge and practice of arcane magic. It must be found, learned from a dying sorcerer, extracted from a grimoire, or paid for in ways that leave marks. Those who go looking for it rarely find something clean.

| Tier | Bonus | Ability | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic | +2 | — | Minor and Moderate spells. The magic is crude, costly, and unreliable. But it works. |
| Advanced | +4 | Major Spells | Unlocks Major spell tier. Corruption rolls become a permanent feature of your existence. The mark is already in you. |
| Mastery | +6 | Forbidden Access (passive) | Forbidden spells become accessible. They will destroy you eventually. You know this. You do it anyway. |

**Advancing to Advanced tier triggers a Corruption roll.** See MAGIC.md (§ Arcane Corruption) for the full table.

**Synergies:**
- **Arcana + Lore → Esoteric:** Rolls to identify magical phenomena are made at advantage.
- **Arcana + Alchemy → Reagent Craft:** Alchemical compounds can be used as spell foci, reducing arcane casting cost by one tier once per scene.
- **Arcana + Medicine → Arcane Anatomy:** May identify magically-induced injuries and the school of magic that caused them.

## Starter Packages

Suggested starting skill packages for common character backgrounds. Players may take any 2–3 skills at Level 1 regardless of these suggestions — these are offered for speed of play.

| Background | Skills | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Frontier Soldier | Blade, Shield & Spear, Endurance | The wall that keeps the dead out. Durable, reliable, uncomplicated. |
| Scout | Stealth, Tracking, Archery | Eyes forward. Never stays where it can be found. |
| Frontier Physician | Herbalism, Medicine, Wilderness Survival | Keeps people alive in places that want them dead. |
